# Unit 1 Quiz — Algebra

**15 questions, 25 minutes, Desmos allowed.** Simulate test conditions. Questions 13–15 are student-produced response (no choices). Answers at the bottom — no peeking.

---

**1.** If 5x + 3 = 2x + 18, what is x?
A) 3  B) 5  C) 7  D) 15

**2.** What is the slope of the line through (−3, 2) and (1, 10)?
A) 1/2  B) 2  C) −2  D) 3

**3.** The graph of y = −3x + 12 crosses the x-axis at
A) (0, 12)  B) (−4, 0)  C) (4, 0)  D) (12, 0)

**4.** If y = x − 4 and 2x + y = 11, what is the value of y?
A) 1  B) 5  C) −1  D) 4

**5.** Which describes all solutions of 4 − 3x ≥ 19?
A) x ≥ −5  B) x ≤ −5  C) x ≥ 5  D) x ≤ 5

**6.** A linear function f satisfies f(1) = 5 and f(4) = 14. What is f(0)?
A) 0  B) 2  C) 3  D) 5

**7.** For what value of k does the system 2x + 5y = 9, 4x + ky = 7 have no solution?
A) 5  B) 7  C) 10  D) 20

**8.** Adult tickets cost $9 and student tickets $5. If 18 tickets were sold for $122 total, how many were adult tickets?
A) 6  B) 8  C) 10  D) 12

**9.** The population of a town is modeled by P(t) = 150 + 12t, where t is years after 2020. The number 12 best represents:
A) the population in 2020
B) the total population increase from 2020 to year t
C) the increase in population each year
D) the number of years for the population to double

**10.** Line m is perpendicular to the line 3x − y = 4 and passes through (6, 1). What is the y-intercept of line m?
A) 3  B) −1  C) 1  D) 6

**11.** How many integers x satisfy −3 ≤ 2x + 1 < 9?
A) 5  B) 6  C) 7  D) 8

**12.** If 2x − 3y = 7, what is the value of 4x − 6y?
A) 3.5  B) 14  C) 21  D) 28

**13.** *(SPR)* A line passes through (2, 3) and (6, 11). If the line is the graph of f, what is f(9)?

**14.** *(SPR)* An elevator has a weight limit of 1200 pounds. A 180-pound worker rides with boxes weighing 55 pounds each. What is the maximum number of boxes the elevator can hold with the worker inside?

**15.** *(SPR)* The system ax + by = 12, 2x + 3y = 6 has infinitely many solutions. What is a + b?

## Answer Key

| # | Answer | Why |
|---|--------|-----|
| 1 | B | 3x = 15 |
| 2 | B | 8/4 = 2 |
| 3 | C | y = 0 → x = 4 |
| 4 | A | 2x + (x − 4) = 11 → x = 5, y = 1 |
| 5 | B | −3x ≥ 15 → x ≤ −5 (flip!) |
| 6 | B | slope 3; back up from (1, 5) → f(0) = 2 |
| 7 | C | k/5 = 4/2 → k = 10; constants 7 ≠ 18 ✓ |
| 8 | B | 9a + 5(18 − a) = 122 → 4a = 32 |
| 9 | C | slope = per-year rate |
| 10 | A | given slope 3 → perp slope −1/3; y = −(1/3)(x − 6) + 1 → b = 3 |
| 11 | B | −2 ≤ x < 4 → {−2, −1, 0, 1, 2, 3} |
| 12 | B | double both sides |
| 13 | 17 | slope 2 → f(x) = 2x − 1 → f(9) = 17 |
| 14 | 18 | 55b + 180 ≤ 1200 → b ≤ 18.5 → 18 |
| 15 | 10 | scale ×2: a = 4, b = 6 |
