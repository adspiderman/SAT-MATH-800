# Lesson 0.4 — Diagnostic Test

**30 questions, 45 minutes, calculator (Desmos) allowed throughout.** Simulate test conditions: paper for scratch work, no pausing, answer everything. Then score with the [answer key](05-diagnostic-answer-key.md) — it maps every question to a course unit so you know exactly where to focus.

Write answers on paper. For multiple choice, letters; otherwise, the value.

---

## Section A — Algebra (Q1–10)

**1.** If 3x − 7 = 14, what is the value of x?
A) 3  B) 7  C) 7/3  D) 21

**2.** A line has slope 3 and passes through (2, 5). Which point also lies on the line?
A) (3, 9)  B) (4, 11)  C) (0, 2)  D) (1, 3)

**3.** If 2x + y = 7 and x − y = 2, what is the value of x + y?

**4.** Which of the following describes all solutions of 5 − 2x > 11?
A) x > −3  B) x < −3  C) x > 3  D) x < 3

**5.** If f(x) = −2x + 9, what is f(−3)?

**6.** A phone plan costs $20 per month plus $0.10 per minute of calls. If a month's bill was $26, how many minutes were used?

**7.** What is the slope of the line through (−1, 4) and (3, −4)?
A) −2  B) 2  C) −1/2  D) 1/2

**8.** For what value of k does the system below have infinitely many solutions?
3x + 2y = 5
6x + 4y = k

**9.** Which equation represents the line through the origin perpendicular to y = ½x + 3?
A) y = ½x  B) y = 2x  C) y = −2x  D) y = −½x

**10.** A tank contains 240 gallons and drains at a constant 15 gallons per hour. After how many hours is the tank empty?

## Section B — Advanced Math (Q11–20)

**11.** What is the sum of the solutions of x² − 5x + 6 = 0?

**12.** Which expression is equivalent to (x³y²)² · x⁻⁴?
A) x²y⁴  B) x¹⁰y⁴  C) x²y²  D) y⁴/x²

**13.** What is the vertex of the parabola y = (x − 3)² − 4?
A) (3, 4)  B) (−3, −4)  C) (3, −4)  D) (−3, 4)

**14.** If √(2x + 5) = 3, what is x?

**15.** A population of 100 bacteria doubles every 5 hours. How many bacteria are there after 15 hours?

**16.** If f(x) = x² + 1 and g(x) = 2x, what is f(g(2))?

**17.** For what value of c does x² + 6x + c = 0 have exactly one real solution?

**18.** For x ≠ −3, which is equivalent to (x² − 9)/(x + 3)?
A) x − 3  B) x + 3  C) x − 9  D) x² − 3

**19.** What is the sum of all solutions of |2x − 1| = 7?

**20.** The graphs of y = x² and y = x + 2 intersect at two points. What is the sum of the x-coordinates of those points?

## Section C — Problem-Solving & Data Analysis (Q21–25)

**21.** Two numbers are in the ratio 3:5 and their sum is 40. What is the larger number?

**22.** A jacket priced at $80 has its price increased by 25%. What is the new price?

**23.** The mean of five numbers is 12. Four of them are 10, 11, 13, and 14. What is the fifth?

**24.** A bag has 4 red and 6 blue marbles. One marble is drawn at random. What is the probability it is red?

**25.** A car travels at 90 kilometers per hour. What is its speed in meters per second?

## Section D — Geometry & Trigonometry (Q26–30)

**26.** Two angles of a triangle measure 35° and 65°. What is the third angle?

**27.** A right triangle has legs of length 6 and 8. What is the length of the hypotenuse?

**28.** What is the radius of the circle x² + y² = 25?

**29.** In a right triangle, sin θ = 3/5 for acute angle θ. What is cos θ?

**30.** What is the volume of a right circular cylinder with radius 3 and height 5? (Answer in terms of π.)
