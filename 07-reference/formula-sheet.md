## Algebra

- Slope: m = (y₂ − y₁)/(x₂ − x₁)
- Slope-intercept: y = mx + b · Point-slope: y − y₁ = m(x − x₁)
- Standard form Ax + By = C: slope = −A/B, x-int = C/A, y-int = C/B
- Parallel: equal slopes · Perpendicular: slopes multiply to −1
- Systems ax+by=c: one solution (slopes differ) / none (a₁/a₂ = b₁/b₂ ≠ c₁/c₂) / infinite (all ratios equal)
- Distance: √((Δx)² + (Δy)²) · Midpoint: ((x₁+x₂)/2, (y₁+y₂)/2)

## Advanced Math

- Quadratic formula: x = (−b ± √(b² − 4ac))/(2a)
- Discriminant b² − 4ac: >0 two real, =0 one, <0 none
- **Vieta:** sum of roots = −b/a, product = c/a
- Vertex: x = −b/(2a); vertex form y = a(x − h)² + k → vertex (h, k)
- Factored form y = a(x − p)(x − q) → axis x = (p + q)/2
- (a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b² · (a − b)² = a² − 2ab + b² · a² − b² = (a+b)(a−b)
- a³ − b³ = (a − b)(a² + ab + b²) · a³ + b³ = (a + b)(a² − ab + b²)
- Exponents: xᵃxᵇ = xᵃ⁺ᵇ · (xᵃ)ᵇ = xᵃᵇ · x⁻ᵃ = 1/xᵃ · x^(a/b) = ᵇ√(xᵃ) · x⁰ = 1
- Exponential model: y = a·bˣ (a = start, b = 1 ± r) · doubling: a·2^(t/k) · half-life: a·(½)^(t/k)
- Remainder theorem: p(x) ÷ (x − a) leaves p(a) · Factor (x − a) ⇔ p(a) = 0 ⇔ x-intercept at a
- Transformations: f(x)+k up · f(x+k) LEFT · −f(x) flip over x-axis · f(−x) flip over y-axis

## Problem-Solving & Data Analysis

- part = percent × whole · % change = (new − old)/old × 100
- Multipliers: +r% → ×(1 + r/100); −r% → ×(1 − r/100); successive changes multiply
- Mean = total/count (work with totals!) · median = middle of sorted · range = max − min
- Weighted mean = combined total ÷ combined count
- Average speed = total distance ÷ total time
- P(event) = favorable/total · P(A|B) = (A and B)/B ← denominator is the "given" group
- Larger sample → smaller margin of error · random sampling → generalize · random assignment → causation

## Geometry & Trigonometry

- Triangle angles sum 180° ★ · exterior angle = sum of remote interior angles
- Polygon interior sum: (n − 2)·180° · exterior sum: 360°
- Pythagorean: a² + b² = c² ★ · Triples: 3-4-5, 5-12-13, 7-24-25, 8-15-17 (+ multiples)
- 45-45-90: x, x, x√2 ★ · 30-60-90: x, x√3, 2x ★
- SOHCAHTOA: sin = opp/hyp, cos = adj/hyp, tan = opp/adj
- **sin θ = cos(90° − θ)**
- sin 30 = cos 60 = 1/2 · sin 45 = cos 45 = √2/2 · sin 60 = cos 30 = √3/2 · tan 45 = 1
- Degrees ↔ radians: × π/180 (→ rad) or × 180/π (→ deg)
- Circle: (x − h)² + (y − k)² = r² · C = 2πr ★ · A = πr² ★
- Arc length = (θ/360)·2πr (= rθ in radians) · Sector area = (θ/360)·πr²
- Inscribed angle = ½ central angle · tangent ⊥ radius
- Areas: triangle ½bh ★ · rectangle ℓw ★ · trapezoid ½(b₁+b₂)h · parallelogram bh
- Volumes ★: box ℓwh · cylinder πr²h · sphere (4/3)πr³ · cone (1/3)πr²h · pyramid (1/3)ℓwh
- Similar figures: lengths ×k ⇒ areas ×k² ⇒ volumes ×k³

## The five facts that win hard questions

1. Vieta's formulas (sum/product of roots without solving)
2. Discriminant = 0 ⇔ tangency ⇔ "exactly one solution"
3. sin θ = cos(90° − θ)
4. Remainder theorem: evaluate, don't divide
5. (x + y)² = x² + 2xy + y² — recognize it disguised (given x² + y² and xy, find x + y)
