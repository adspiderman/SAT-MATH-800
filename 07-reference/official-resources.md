# Official & Supplementary Resources

This course teaches all tested content, tactics, and provides practice — but **official College Board questions are the only perfectly calibrated ones.** Use these alongside the course, at the milestones in your [study plan](../00-orientation/03-study-plans.md).

## Official (College Board) — use all of these

| Resource | What | Where |
|----------|------|-------|
| **Bluebook app** | The real testing app with full-length official adaptive practice tests (the single most important external resource) | [bluebook.collegeboard.org](https://bluebook.collegeboard.org) |
| **SAT Practice on Khan Academy** | Free official question bank with lessons, organized by skill and difficulty | [khanacademy.org/sat](https://www.khanacademy.org/sat) |
| **College Board Question Bank** | Thousands of official questions filterable by domain/skill/difficulty — build custom hard-question drills | [satsuitequestionbank.collegeboard.org](https://satsuitequestionbank.collegeboard.org) |
| **Desmos SAT testing calculator** | The exact calculator embedded in Bluebook | [desmos.com/testing/cb-digital-sat/graphing](https://www.desmos.com/testing/cb-digital-sat/graphing) |
| **SAT dates & registration** | Deadlines and test dates | [satsuite.collegeboard.org](https://satsuite.collegeboard.org/sat/registration) |

## How to weave official material into this course

1. **Question Bank drills:** after finishing each course unit, pull 15–20 official questions from that domain (filter: hardest difficulty for Units 1–2 after the quiz goes well). This is the best supplementary volume available, free.
2. **Bluebook tests at milestones only** (they're limited in number — currently ~6): after Phase 1, after Phase 2, and in the final week. Don't burn them early.
3. **Khan Academy** for extra reps on any lesson where the course practice set wasn't enough.

## Score reporting reality

- Digital SAT scores arrive in days, not weeks.
- Superscoring: most colleges take your best section scores across sittings — a weaker math day can be retaken without losing a strong verbal score.

## A note on third-party books/apps

Nothing third-party (including this course's practice tests) perfectly replicates official question calibration. The hierarchy: **official questions > this course's lessons and tactics > any other third-party volume.** Use this course to learn *how to think*; use official questions to calibrate.
