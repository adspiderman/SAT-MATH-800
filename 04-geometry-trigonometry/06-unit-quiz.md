# Unit 4 Quiz — Geometry & Trigonometry

**12 questions, 18 minutes, Desmos allowed.** Questions 11–12 are student-produced response.

---

**1.** Lines ℓ and m are parallel. A transversal makes a 74° angle with ℓ. What is the measure of the alternate interior angle at m?
A) 16°  B) 74°  C) 106°  D) 148°

**2.** The angles of a triangle measure 2x°, 3x°, and 4x°. What is the largest angle?
A) 20°  B) 40°  C) 60°  D) 80°

**3.** A right triangle has legs 5 and 12. What is the hypotenuse?
A) 13  B) 15  C) 17  D) √119

**4.** In a right triangle, sin θ = 8/17. What is tan θ?
A) 15/17  B) 8/15  C) 15/8  D) 17/15

**5.** If sin(x + 10)° = cos 40° and 0 < x < 80, what is x?
A) 30  B) 40  C) 50  D) 60

**6.** What are the center and radius of (x + 1)² + (y − 3)² = 49?
A) (1, −3), r = 7  B) (−1, 3), r = 7  C) (−1, 3), r = 49  D) (1, −3), r = 49

**7.** A circle has radius 9. What is the length of an arc with a 40° central angle?
A) π  B) 2π  C) 4π  D) 9π

**8.** Two similar triangles have corresponding sides in ratio 2:5. The smaller has area 12. What is the larger's area?
A) 30  B) 48  C) 60  D) 75

**9.** A 30-60-90 triangle has hypotenuse 16. What is the length of the longer leg?
A) 8  B) 8√2  C) 8√3  D) 16√3

**10.** What is the volume of a sphere with radius 6?
A) 96π  B) 144π  C) 288π  D) 864π

**11.** *(SPR)* In triangle ABC, points D and E are on sides AB and AC with DE ∥ BC. AD = 3, DB = 9, and BC = 16. What is DE?

**12.** *(SPR)* The equation x² + y² − 8x + 6y = 11 describes a circle. What is its radius?

---

## Answer Key

| # | Answer | Why |
|---|--------|-----|
| 1 | B | alternate interior angles are equal |
| 2 | D | 9x = 180 → x = 20 → 4x = 80 |
| 3 | A | 5-12-13 triple |
| 4 | B | 8-15-17 triple; opp 8, adj 15 |
| 5 | B | (x + 10) + 40 = 90 |
| 6 | B | signs flip; r = √49 |
| 7 | B | (40/360)(18π) |
| 8 | D | area ratio (5/2)² = 25/4; 12 × 25/4 = 75 |
| 9 | C | short leg 8; long leg 8√3 |
| 10 | C | (4/3)π(216) |
| 11 | 4 | ratio AD/AB = 3/12 → DE = 16/4 |
| 12 | 6 | (x−4)² + (y+3)² = 11 + 16 + 9 = 36 |
