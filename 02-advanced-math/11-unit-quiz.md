# Unit 2 Quiz — Advanced Math

**15 questions, 25 minutes, Desmos allowed.** Questions 13–15 are student-produced response.

---

**1.** Which is equivalent to (2x²y)³?
A) 2x⁶y³  B) 6x⁶y³  C) 8x⁶y³  D) 8x⁵y³

**2.** What is the sum of the solutions of x² − 9x + 18 = 0?
A) 3  B) 6  C) 9  D) 18

**3.** What is the vertex of y = x² + 8x + 10?
A) (4, 58)  B) (−4, −6)  C) (−4, 10)  D) (4, −6)

**4.** What is the solution of √(x + 7) = x − 5?
A) 2  B) 9  C) 2 and 9  D) No solution

**5.** If 4^(x+1) = 8ˣ, what is x?
A) 1  B) 2  C) 3  D) 4

**6.** If f(x) = 2 · 3ˣ, what is f(4)/f(2)?
A) 2  B) 3  C) 6  D) 9

**7.** The equation x² + kx + 25 = 0 has exactly one real solution and k > 0. What is k?
A) 5  B) 10  C) 25  D) 50

**8.** For x ≠ −5, which is equivalent to (x² + 3x − 10)/(x + 5)?
A) x − 2  B) x + 2  C) x − 5  D) x + 3

**9.** What is the product of the solutions of |5 − 2x| = 3?
A) −4  B) 4  C) 5  D) −5

**10.** The function f has a minimum at (1, 4). What is the minimum point of g(x) = f(x + 3) − 2?
A) (4, 2)  B) (−2, 2)  C) (4, 6)  D) (−2, 6)

**11.** What is the remainder when p(x) = 2x³ − x² + 3x − 5 is divided by x − 2?
A) 0  B) 9  C) 13  D) 17

**12.** What is the minimum value of y = (x − 3)(x + 5)?
A) −16  B) −15  C) −1  D) −8

**13.** *(SPR)* If 3^(2x) = 81, what is x?

**14.** *(SPR)* The graph of y = x² − 6x + k is tangent to the x-axis. What is k?

**15.** *(SPR)* What is the sum of the x-coordinates of the solutions of the system y = x² − 3x, y = x?

## Answer Key

| # | Answer | Why |
|---|--------|-----|
| 1 | C | 2³ = 8; (x²)³ = x⁶ |
| 2 | C | −b/a = 9 (roots 3 and 6) |
| 3 | B | x = −8/2 = −4; y = 16 − 32 + 10 = −6 |
| 4 | B | squaring gives x² − 11x + 18 = 0 → x = 2, 9; x = 2 fails the original (√9 = 3 ≠ −3) |
| 5 | B | 2^(2x+2) = 2^(3x) → 2x + 2 = 3x |
| 6 | D | 3⁴/3² = 9 |
| 7 | B | D = k² − 100 = 0 → k = 10 |
| 8 | A | (x + 5)(x − 2)/(x + 5) |
| 9 | B | 5 − 2x = ±3 → x = 1, 4 → product 4 |
| 10 | B | left 3, down 2 from (1, 4) |
| 11 | C | p(2) = 16 − 4 + 6 − 5 = 13 |
| 12 | A | roots 3, −5 → vertex at x = −1 → y = (−4)(4) = −16 |
| 13 | 2 | 3^(2x) = 3⁴ |
| 14 | 9 | tangent ⇔ D = 36 − 4k = 0 |
| 15 | 4 | x² − 4x = 0 → x = 0, 4 |
